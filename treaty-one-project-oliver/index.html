What is Treaty 1?

Treaty 1 was the first numbered treaty that was signed in Canada in 1871 as an agreement for them to share the land with First Nations people and give them special benefits. This laid the groundwork for all of the rest of the numbered treaties in Canada. This treaty was signed in the "stone fort" which is Lower Fort Garry in modern-day Manitoba. This is the treaty that I, the person making this website, resides on and also all the people living in Winnipeg.
